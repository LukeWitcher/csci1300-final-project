
# The Final Bundle

## Theme
This game is set in Stardew Valley. You play as the newest farmer in town, and the Community Center is one bundle away from being fully restored.

## Goal
Complete the final Community Center bundle before the season ends. Along the way you'll travel between locations, talk to villagers, collect items, manage your energy and money, and decide whether to take JojaMart's shortcuts or rely on the community to finish the bundle.
